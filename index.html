A range of services provided by SSC

SSC are roofing, cladding, metal fabrication specialists covering London, and all surrounding areas. Same team from design to completion, whether it be the full roofing and cladding package, over cladding a building, maintenance and refurbishment work, finishing and flashing works.

Green roofing

Sustainable & eco-friendly surface attracting a variety of wildlife and helping with water retention. High u-value performance.

Composite Panels

Cost effective, quick install, all in one insulated panel. Many profiles and finishes to choose from for both roof and walls.

Liquid waterproofing

The process of waterproofing a roof by the application of a specialist liquid coating. It is suited to most types of roof.
